PRECAUTIONS FOR USE

  • Width can vary slightly when measuring.
  • Exposure to heat (high-temperature steam, ironing/pressing, etc.) may cause discoloration, deformation, or migration. Perform a preliminary test with a sample cloth or other materials before use.
  • Please conduct your own tests for shrinkage as well as sewing to avoid sewing errors and seam puckering in bulk production. After conducting your own tests, please apply appropriate care label to the garment.
  • Items dyed in deep colors may stain lighter fabrics. We thank you for your understanding in this matter.
  • Relax spooled items for at least 24 hours before use to avoid shrinkage.
  • To avoid discoloration and brittleness, avoid contact with chlorine bleach.

add rubber thread

  • To avoid deterioration of natural rubber threads, please keep away from high temperatures/humidity.
  • Items containing natural rubber thread may turn yellow when exposed to nitrogen oxides.
