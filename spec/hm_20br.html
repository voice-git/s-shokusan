PRECAUTIONS FOR USE

  • Tape rolls with the same color number may have different hues depending on the production lot.
  • Do not leave tape in a wet condition for a long time, as color bleeding or color transfer may occur. If tape becomes wet, dry it promptly.
  • If the percentage of contraction for this stretch binder tape is different from that for the material it is applied to, creases may occur. Check the contraction percentages for both tape and material in advance, and test them for conformity.
  • Leave the tape for 12hours or longer after it is unrolled.
